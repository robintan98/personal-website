About Me


I am a sophomore in the Management and Technology Program, a competitive dual-degree program between the School of Engineering and Applied Science and the Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania, where I am studying Computer Science and Finance with a minor in Mathematics.


I am enthusiastic about pursuing software engineering, data analytics, and product management after graduation. Through my local state university, I have conducted a total of 6 research projects in machine learning and artificial intelligence and have been published in numerous international conferences and journals. In addition, I am huge robotics geek: I had the honor of co-captaining the world's first and only 2x World Champion FIRST Tech Challenge robotics team. I also love dabbling in coding projects, and I have worked with companies ranging from small startups to industry-leading giants.


I am originally from Beaverton, Oregon, where I was the Valedictorian of Jesuit High School. At Jesuit, I co-led our school's History Bowl team, co-led a STEM-for-youth volunteer organization, and volunteered frequently at a local retirement home. My hobbies include teaching kids, playing poker, and hiking. In my free time, I love getting my hands dirty with do-it-yourself building projects as well as meeting new people :0!
